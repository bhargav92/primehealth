Corporate Health

Welcome to Primehealthin, your premier partner in corporate health services. We specialize in providing comprehensive health solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of businesses and their employees. Our mission is to enhance workplace well-being through a range of services, including pre-employment health checks, annual health checks, corporate wellness programs, occupational health services, and health talks by renowned medical professionals.

After conducting onsite or offsite health checks, we present a detailed health status report for corporate employees. We share all critical and abnormal employee data with HR/Admin to ensure prompt action. Our services don’t end there—we arrange specialty and super-specialty doctor consultations and provide spectacles/eyewear for employees with vision abnormalities. Similarly, for employees with profound hearing issues, we supply hearing aids at competitive prices on demand. We are also specialized in organizing FDA-approved Pharma plants health checks.

Primehealthin is your one-stop solution for all corporate healthcare needs
