About the package

The Elderly Female Health Package is a comprehensive screening program designed to address the unique health needs of women aged 60 and above. This package aims to detect potential health issues early, enabling timely intervention and promoting overall well-being.

12 Lab Tests in this package
  • Complete Urine Examination
  • Liver Function Test (LFT)
  • Glycosylated Haemoglobin (HbA1C)
  • Fasting Blood Glucose
  • Lipid Profile
  • Blood Urea Nitrogen (BUN)
  • Creatinine
  • Calcium
  • Thyroid Stimulating Hormone (TSH)
  • Complete Blood Count
  • Phosphorus
  • Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate
